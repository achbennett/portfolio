Grant Attack!

Grants are made up of a number of moving pieces. In order to process a grant, you have to bring all those pieces together - just like in Grant Attack.

Use the left and right arrow keys to move pieces to the desired position on your desk. The up arrow key rotates pieces. Completing a row finishes a grant and moves it to the "Processed" box. Fill up your "Processed" box to get to the next level.

Don't let your grants stack up, or it'll be game over!
